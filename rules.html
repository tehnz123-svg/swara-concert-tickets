Rules & Regulations

Please read the following guidelines carefully before attending the concert

Event Guidelines
  • Each ticket is valid for one person only. SA number will be verified at the entrance against the university database.
  • Tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Please carry your valid student ID along with the e-ticket for verification at the entrance.
  • Entry will be denied without valid SA number and photo identification.
  • Doors open at 5:30 PM. The event starts promptly at 6:30 PM. Latecomers will be admitted only during suitable breaks.
  • Professional cameras, video recorders, and audio recording devices are strictly prohibited.
  • Outside food and beverages are not allowed inside the venue. Food stalls will be available inside.
  • The organizers reserve the right to perform security checks at the entrance for safety purposes.
  • Any form of misconduct, unruly behavior, or violation of venue rules will lead to immediate expulsion from the venue without refund.
  • Please follow all COVID-19 safety protocols as mandated by the university and local authorities.
  • Smoking and consumption of alcohol is strictly prohibited within the venue premises.
  • The management reserves the right to deny entry or remove any person from the venue without assigning any reason.
  • Children below 12 years are not permitted for this event.
  • All attendees consent to photography, audio recording, and video recording to be used for promotional purposes.

Important Note

By purchasing a ticket and attending the event, you agree to comply with all the rules and regulations stated above. The organizers reserve the right to modify these rules at any time without prior notice.
